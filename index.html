FAQ

Common questions about Meeting Mayhem

What is Meeting Mayhem?

Meeting Mayhem is a casual calendar puzzle game for Android. Each level gives you a busy schedule with overlapping meetings; you drag blocks into open time slots, work around locked events, and clear the board using quick, satisfying moves.

Is Meeting Mayhem free?

Yes. Meeting Mayhem is free to download and play on Google Play. The game is ad-supported, with optional in-game rewards and daily goals to keep sessions short and fun.

Which devices run Meeting Mayhem?

Meeting Mayhem is built for Android phones and tablets and is distributed through the Google Play Store. Install it on any compatible Android device that meets the listing requirements on Google Play.

Where can I download Meeting Mayhem?

Download Meeting Mayhem from Google Play at https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.BlueberryBasement.MeetingMayhem — or use the Get it on Google Play button on this page for the same store listing.
